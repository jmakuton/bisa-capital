About the Fund

Real assets. Measurable impact.

BISA Capital I deploys equity into infrastructure projects that contribute to Poland's energy transition. The fund is structured as a Polish ASI — a regulated alternative investment vehicle — and invests alongside local debt financing into real assets with tangible economic and environmental outcomes.

Regulated Structure

Polish Alternative Investment Fund under institutional-grade governance.

EUR Denominated

Fund operates in EUR with local currency exposure managed at project level.

Real Assets

Every euro of equity is deployed into physical infrastructure with measurable output.

Impact Aligned

Investments contribute directly to Poland's energy transition and climate objectives.

Investment Focus

Energy & infrastructure in Poland

The fund targets a diversified set of infrastructure assets across the Polish energy transition landscape.
